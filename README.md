## What Is Encharge, Actually?

At its core, Encharge lets you build automated email workflows triggered by what your users *actually do* — not just "they signed up" or "it's Tuesday at 10am." We're talking about things like: a user visited your pricing page three times in 24 hours, a Stripe payment just went through, or someone clicked a specific link in an email and then ghosted your app for a week.

That kind of behavior-based logic is what makes the difference between email that converts and email that lands in the promotions tab alongside 47 other newsletters nobody asked for.

The visual flow builder is the centerpiece. You drag and drop triggers, conditions, actions, delays, and email steps onto a canvas. Connect them with lines. Hit publish. It's genuinely that clean — no developer needed, no consulting engagement required.

Beyond automation flows, Encharge covers broadcasts (your regular newsletters and announcements), A/B testing for both individual emails and entire flows, lead scoring, site tracking, native forms, and deep integrations with tools like Stripe, HubSpot, Salesforce, Segment, Shopify, WooCommerce, Calendly, Typeform, Facebook Ads, Zapier, and 40+ more.

## A Few Things Worth Knowing Before You Buy

The lifetime deal does *not* stack — you can't combine multiple license tiers into one larger account. If you think you'll need more subscriber capacity in the future, it's worth buying the right tier now rather than later.

The maximum subscriber count under the AppSumo lifetime deal is 50,000 (Tier 6). Beyond that, you'd pay for overages at a 50% discount off regular Growth pricing — so the math still works in your favor.

Transactional emails (password resets, payment receipts) are a Premium plan feature and are not included in the lifetime Growth plan tiers. If you need that functionality specifically, plan accordingly.

Cold/outbound email is not permitted. Encharge requires that your contacts have opted in. If that's your use case, look elsewhere.
